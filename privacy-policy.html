Privacy Policy - BoilerNN

Last updated: January 26, 2025

1. Information We Collect

BoilerNN is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information necessary to provide our educational image analysis service:

  • Images: Photos you choose to analyze are temporarily processed for mathematical and scientific analysis
  • Usage Analytics: Basic app usage statistics to improve our service (anonymized)
  • Purchase Information: Subscription status managed by Apple (we don't store payment details)

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Provide AI-powered mathematical and scientific image analysis
  • Improve our educational algorithms and user experience
  • Manage your subscription status
  • Provide customer support when requested

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Temporary Processing: Images are processed temporarily and not permanently stored
  • Encryption: All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Limited Access: Only authorized systems access your data for processing
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We don't sell or share your personal data with third parties

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access information about your data usage
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of non-essential data collection
  • Contact us about privacy concerns

5. Children's Privacy

BoilerNN is designed for educational purposes and may be used by students. We comply with COPPA and similar children's privacy laws by:

  • Not collecting personal information from children under 13
  • Processing only educational content (mathematical/scientific images)
  • Not storing personal profiles or persistent user data

6. International Users

BoilerNN complies with international privacy regulations including GDPR (EU) and CCPA (California). Users outside the United States have the same privacy protections and rights described in this policy.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Users will be notified of significant changes through the app or this website.

8. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on:

  • Legitimate Interest: Providing educational analysis services
  • Contract Performance: Fulfilling our service agreement with users
  • Consent: Where explicitly provided for optional features

Contact Information

For privacy-related questions or requests:

Email: privacy@boilernn.com

Response Time: We respond to privacy inquiries within 30 days

This privacy policy is effective as of the last updated date and applies to all users of BoilerNN.
